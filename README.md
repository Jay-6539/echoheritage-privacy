Echo Heritage App Privacy Policy
Effective Date: November 11, 2025
Last Updated: November 11, 2025
Echo Heritage ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS app ("App"), which involves NFC scanning of historic buildings, user-generated threads (messages), AI rating with Qwen, NFT minting on Polygon blockchain via Alchemy, ECT token economy, user levels, daily quests, likes, arena comparisons, and building HP growth. By using the App, you consent to the practices described here.
We comply with the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are under 13, please get parental consent.
1. Information We Collect
We collect minimal data necessary for App functionality:

Personal Information: Email or social login (for user account, levels, quests, and ECT balance).
User Content: Threads (messages you submit), including text, timestamp, and building ID (for AI rating and NFT minting).
Location Data: Approximate location via NFC scan (for building interaction and HP growth; not precise GPS).
Blockchain Data: NFT addresses, token IDs, and transaction hashes (for minting, likes, arena comparisons, and family inheritance).
Usage Data: Interactions (scans, likes, bids, arena swipes) for quests, XP, and Elo rankings.
Device Info: IP address, device ID (for fraud prevention, anonymized).

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., health, religion) unless voluntarily in threads.

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Features: Rate threads with Qwen AI, mint NFTs on Polygon (via Alchemy), track XP/levels/quests, enable likes/arena comparisons, and grow building HP.
Personalization: Recommend threads/NFTs based on interactions (e.g., family inheritance suggestions).
Rewards: Distribute ECT tokens for quests, XP gains, and building contributions (backend matching, no direct deduction).
Analytics: Improve App (e.g., Elo rankings for content curation) using aggregated data.
Security: Detect fraud (e.g., daily limits on threads/likes).

We do not use data for advertising or unrelated purposes.

Service Providers: With Alchemy (NFT minting), Qwen (AI rating), Firebase (database for quests/HP), and Polygon blockchain (public transactions).
No Selling: We do not sell data.
Legal: Only if required by law (e.g., PDPO compliance).
Business Transfers: In app sale/merger, with notice.

Opt-Out: Disable tracking in settings.
Children: No intentional collection from under 13; parents contact us.
Retention: Delete inactive data after 2 years.
